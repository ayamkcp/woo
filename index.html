About Me

Woo Won-jae gained attention as a participant on Show Me The Money 6, where he finished third place. He released his first single "We Are (시차)" featuring Gray and Loco on September 4, 2017, following his elimination from Show Me The Money 6. On October 31, 2017, it was announced that he had signed with record label AOMG. He released his first single under AOMG, titled "Anxiety (불안)", on November 2, 2017.
